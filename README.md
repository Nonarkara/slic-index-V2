# SLIC Index V2 — The Taipei Launch

**Launched at Smart City Summit & Expo 2026, Taipei.** 3,000 professionals. The Vice President of Taiwan opened the event. A European mayor's alliance asked to use SLIC instead of The Economist's index.

174 cities. 53 countries. The first interactive city ranking where you drag a spider web to rebuild the top 10 based on your own priorities.

## What This Version Introduced

- **Interactive spider diagram** — drag vertices to reweight the 5 pillars in real time
- **174 cities** across 53 countries (up from 103 in V1)
- **Consequence rules** — the system tells you what your weight choices imply ("Silicon Valley syndrome", "Nordic model")
- **City scorecards** — click any city to see exactly where every number comes from
- **Thailand provincial rankings** — 77 Thai provinces scored
